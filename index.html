Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

As a pioneer in mobile financial services, bKash revolutionized financial inclusion in Bangladesh, making banking accessible to millions and inspiring fintech innovations worldwide.

Sustainability

Home to the world's highest number of green garment factories, Bangladesh is redefining sustainability in the apparel industry, blending innovation with eco-conscious practices.

Infrastructure

Investments in urban infrastructure and smart city initiatives are reshaping Dhaka and Chattogram, addressing challenges of urbanization with technology-driven solutions.

Empowerment

The nation's focus on digital literacy and freelancing opportunities is empowering its youth, making Bangladesh one of the fastest-growing contributors to the global gig economy.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
